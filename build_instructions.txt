to build with the multifast lib is really strange

here is how you can make example 3 work:
g++ -o example3.o -c example3.cpp -I./multifast-ac/ahocorasick -Wall
g++ -o AhoCorasickPlus.o -c AhoCorasickPlus.cpp -I./multifast-ac/ahocorasick -Wall
g++ -o example3 example3.o AhoCorasickPlus.o -lahocorasick -L./multifast-ac/ahocorasick/build

to make the driver:
g++ -o driver.o -c driver.cpp -I./multifast-ac/ahocorasick -Wall
g++ -o AhoCorasickPlus.o -c AhoCorasickPlus.cpp -I./multifast-ac/ahocorasick -Wall
g++ -o paraglob.o -c paraglob.cpp
g++ -o driver driver.o paraglob.o AhoCorasickPlus.o -lahocorasick -L./multifast-ac/ahocorasick/build

to make the bencher:
